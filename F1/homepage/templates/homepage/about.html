ABOUT PROJECT

We would like to present you a Formula One website created in Django framework. This a final project made during the Python bootcamp at Software Development Academy (SDA). You can find here multiple apps like F1 Statistics, Quiz, Events, Blogs and About Us page. You can also check what are the current weather conditions on the next circuit location.
